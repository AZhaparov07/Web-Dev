Travel to Japan!

Why Japan?

Japan is an island country in East Asia. It is known for its rich culture, modern technology, and beautiful traditions. The capital city is Tokyo. Japan combines ancient customs, such as tea ceremonies and temples, with modern life and advanced science. People in Japan value respect, discipline, and hard work.
The country is also famous for sushi, anime, and cherry blossoms.
