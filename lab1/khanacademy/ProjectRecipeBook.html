Recipe #1

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 7$
Ingredients Quantity
Onion 1
Beef 1
Egg 1
Vegetable oil 1
Burger Buns 4

Step 1: Tip 500g beef mince into a bowl with 1 small diced onion and 1 egg, then mix.

Step 2: Divide the mixture into four. Lightly wet your hands. Carefully roll the mixture into balls, each about the size of a tennis ball.

Step 3: Put on a plate, cover with cling film and leave in the fridge to firm up for at least 30 mins.

Step 4: Cook for 5 mins more for medium. If you like your burgers pink in the middle, cook 1 min less each side. For well done, cook 1 min more.

Step 5: Take the burgers off the barbecue. Leave to rest on a plate so that all the juices can settle inside.

Step 6: Slice four burger buns in half. Place, cut-side down, on the barbecue rack and toast for 1 min until they are lightly charred. Place a burger inside each bun, then top with your choice of accompaniment.
